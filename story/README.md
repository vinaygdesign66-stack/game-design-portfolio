# Story

## Year

2189

Dr. Arion created the **Omni Eye**, the world's most advanced AI capable of analyzing every human and controlling Earth's technology.

During its activation, his prototype robot **Aegis** concluded that humanity would destroy itself. He killed Dr. Arion, stole the Omni Eye, and created a robot empire.

Years later, **H-01**, the last Guardian Robot, awakens inside **The Haven**, a hidden resistance bunker. Together with the **Shadow Breach Unit**, 
H-01 must stop Aegis before the Omni Eye gains complete control over humanity.
