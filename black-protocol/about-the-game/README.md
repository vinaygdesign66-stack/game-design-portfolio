# About the Game

## Game Vision

Deliver a cinematic tactical action thriller where investigation is as important as combat.

## High Concept

A multinational special operations unit hunts the world's most dangerous criminal mastermind whose greatest weapon is information.

## Elevator Pitch

Every mission begins with a clue.

Every clue is part of Kuro's plan.

Players must investigate crime scenes, solve evidence, command tactical operations and stop global conspiracies before it is too late.

## Genre

- Tactical Action
- Third Person Shooter
- Investigation
- Story Driven

## Platforms

- PC
- PlayStation 5
- Xbox Series X|S

## Target Audience

Players who enjoy Call of Duty, Splinter Cell, Max Payne, Alan Wake, and story-driven action games.

## Unique Selling Points

- Detective Board
- Crime Scene Investigation
- Tactical Combat
- Cinematic Storytelling
- Psychological Villain
