## Overview

BLACK PROTOCOL is an original story-driven tactical action thriller that combines cinematic storytelling, military operations, detective investigation, stealth gameplay, and large-scale action sequences.

Players take control of **Captain Alex Kane**, commander of the elite multinational special operations unit **Shadow Six**, as they hunt the world's most dangerous criminal mastermind, **Kuro**. Every mission uncovers another layer of a global conspiracy where every clue has been planted deliberately, forcing players to investigate crime scenes, analyze evidence, and outthink an enemy who is always one move ahead.
