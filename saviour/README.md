# 🐺 SAVIOUR

> **"One warrior. One prophecy. One final battle beneath the Tree of Souls."**

---

# Overview

**SAVIOUR** is a story-driven fantasy action-adventure game where players control a divine wolf-headed warrior chosen by prophecy to end the reign of an immortal king.

The game combines cinematic storytelling, fast-paced melee combat, exploration, mythical creatures, puzzle solving, and epic boss battles across a mystical world inspired by ancient mythology.

# Story

A cruel king is reborn with immortality linked to a mysterious **Golden Star** hidden inside the **Tree of Souls**.

A sacred prophecy awakens a wolf-headed warrior through the power of the **Blue Bracelet**.

To save the world, the Saviour must protect a young child carrying the Golden Star, defeat eight immortal crane warriors, collect divine relics across the sacred lands, and finally destroy the Immortal King's curse beneath the Tree of Souls.
