# Gameplay

## Overview

**SAVIOUR** is a third-person mythological action-adventure game that combines fast-paced melee combat, exploration, environmental puzzles, and cinematic boss battles. Players control **The Saviour**, mastering multiple weapons and divine abilities while traveling across ancient lands to defeat the Immortal King.

---

# Combat

Combat is skill-based and encourages players to combine different weapons and abilities.

## Sword Combat

The **Divine Sword** is the primary weapon used throughout the game.

**Features**

* Light Attack Combos
* Heavy Attacks
* Charged Attacks
* Air Attacks
* Combo Finishers
* Divine Strike

---

## Spear Combat

The **Guardian Spear** provides greater reach and crowd control.

**Features**

* Long-Range Thrust
* Wide Sweep Attack
* Spear Throw
* Jump Strike
* Charged Spear Attack

---

## Archery

The **Sacred Bow** is used for long-range combat, hunting, and puzzle solving.

**Features**

* Precision Shooting
* Charged Shots
* Weak Point Attacks
* Fire Arrow
* Ice Arrow
* Explosive Arrow

---

## Knife Combat

The **Golden Knife** is designed for fast close-range combat and stealth.

**Features**

* Quick Slash
* Backstab
* Counter Attack
* Knife Throw
* Silent Elimination

---

## Parry

Players can parry enemy attacks with precise timing.

Successful parries briefly stagger enemies and create opportunities for powerful counterattacks.

---

## Dodge

Quick dodges help avoid enemy attacks.

Players can dodge in any direction to escape dangerous situations and reposition during combat.

---

## Executions

When an enemy's health is critically low, players can perform cinematic finishing moves using the equipped weapon.

---

# Exploration

Exploration rewards players with hidden secrets, upgrades, and lore.

## Horse Riding

Travel across forests, villages, rivers, and ancient ruins using the Saviour's horse.

---

## Climbing

Scale cliffs, temple walls, giant trees, and mountain paths to reach hidden locations.

---

## Swimming

Cross rivers, lakes, flooded caves, and shallow waters while searching for hidden paths and treasures.

---

## Puzzle Solving

Ancient temples contain environmental puzzles involving:

* Pressure Plates
* Moving Statues
* Sacred Symbols
* Light Reflection
* Divine Relics

---

## Hidden Areas

Every mission includes secret locations containing:

* Weapon Upgrades
* Bracelet Upgrades
* Ancient Scrolls
* Collectibles
* Hidden Treasure

---

## Collectibles

Players can discover valuable items throughout the world.

**Collectibles include**

* Ancient Scrolls
* Guru Records
* Divine Relics
* Golden Coins
* Lost Artifacts
* Tree of Souls Fragments

---

# Progression

As the adventure continues, the Saviour becomes stronger by unlocking new equipment and abilities.

## Weapon Upgrades

Upgrade the:

* Divine Sword
* Guardian Spear
* Sacred Bow
* Golden Knife

Weapons gain increased damage, new attack combos, and unique abilities.

---

## Bracelet Upgrades

The **Blue Bracelet** grows stronger throughout the story.

New abilities include:

* Increased Strength
* Faster Movement
* Health Regeneration
* Divine Vision
* Enhanced Combat Power
* Energy Recovery

---

## New Combat Skills

Players unlock powerful abilities as they progress.

Examples include:

* Dash Strike
* Spin Slash
* Spear Cyclone
* Multi-Shot Archery
* Perfect Parry Counter
* Divine Execution

---

## Divine Relics

Sacred relics found during the journey permanently enhance the Saviour's abilities.

**Relics**

* Fire Crystal
* Ice Crystal
* Emerald Fang
* Sky Feather
* Ocean Pearl

Each relic strengthens the Blue Bracelet and unlocks new powers required to complete the adventure.
