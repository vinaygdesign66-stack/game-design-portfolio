# Level Design

## Overview

The world of **SAVIOUR** is designed around a linear, story-driven adventure with open exploration areas. Each level introduces new environments, enemies, puzzles, and boss encounters while gradually expanding the player's abilities.

Every level includes:

* Entry Area
* Exploration Zone
* Combat Encounters
* Hidden Paths
* Puzzle Sections
* Collectibles
* Boss Arena
* Exit Area

---

# Prologue – The Sacred Temple

## Theme

Ancient mountain temple where the Gurus protect the Blue Bracelet.

## Environment

* Temple Courtyard
* Meditation Hall
* Sacred Shrine
* Mountain Cliffs
* Training Grounds

## Objectives

* Learn movement and combat.
* Receive the Blue Bracelet.
* Attempt to lift the Divine Sword.
* Begin the journey.

## Gameplay

* Tutorial
* Basic Combat
* Climbing
* Exploration

## Exit

Leave the temple on horseback.

| --------- | ----------------- | ---------------------------------------------------- | -------------------- |

| Mission 1 | Fallen Village    | Rescue the Child                                     | Crane Commander      |

# Level Design Goals

* Deliver memorable handcrafted environments.
* Encourage exploration through hidden rewards.
* Balance combat, puzzles, and traversal.
* Gradually increase challenge and enemy variety.
* Support cinematic storytelling while giving players multiple paths to explore.
