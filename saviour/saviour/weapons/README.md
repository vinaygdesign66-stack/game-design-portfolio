# Weapons

## Overview

The combat system in **SAVIOUR** is built around four primary weapons. Each weapon has a unique purpose, allowing players to switch between fast melee combat, long-range attacks, defensive fighting, and stealth.

The **Blue Bracelet** enhances these weapons with divine power as the story progresses.

---

# Hero Weapons

## Divine Sword

**Type:** Legendary Great Sword

**Primary User:** The Saviour

### Description

The Divine Sword was forged by the gods and entrusted to the Gurus to await the world's destined saviour. It is the only weapon capable of destroying the Golden Star and defeating the Immortal King and his immortal warriors.

### Combat Style

* Light Attack
* Heavy Attack
* Charged Slash
* Air Attack
* Combo Finishers
* Execution

### Special Ability

**Divine Judgment**

A powerful charged attack that releases divine energy capable of breaking enemy defenses.

---

## Guardian Spear

**Type:** Sacred Spear

**Primary User:** The Saviour

### Description

A long-range melee weapon designed for precision and crowd control. The spear can be thrown and magically recalled through the power of the Blue Bracelet.

### Combat Style

* Forward Thrust
* Wide Sweep
* Jump Strike
* Spear Throw
* Charged Attack

### Special Ability

**Heaven's Spear**

Throws the spear through multiple enemies before returning to the player.

---

## Sacred Bow

**Type:** Long Bow

**Primary User:** The Saviour

### Description

A sacred bow crafted from the ancient Tree of Souls. It allows players to eliminate enemies from long range and solve environmental puzzles.

### Arrow Types

* Standard Arrow
* Fire Arrow
* Ice Arrow
* Explosive Arrow
* Divine Arrow

### Combat Style

* Precision Shots
* Charged Shots
* Weak Point Attacks
* Multi-Shot
* Long-Range Combat

### Special Ability

**Spirit Shot**

Temporarily slows time while aiming, allowing perfect precision.

---

## Golden Knife

**Type:** Sacred Combat Knife

**Primary User:** The Saviour

### Description

A ceremonial golden knife carried as a secondary weapon. It is lightweight, extremely sharp, and ideal for close combat and stealth eliminations.

### Combat Style

* Quick Slash
* Backstab
* Counter Attack
* Knife Throw
* Silent Execution

### Special Ability

**Shadow Strike**

Instantly eliminates unaware enemies during stealth.

---

# Blue Bracelet

## Type

Divine Artifact

### Description

The Blue Bracelet is the source of the Saviour's supernatural abilities. Rather than functioning as a weapon, it enhances movement, combat, and survivability throughout the adventure.

### Abilities

* Enhanced Strength
* Increased Speed
* Health Regeneration
* Divine Vision
* Double Jump
* Dash Ability
* Energy Recovery
* Weapon Enhancement

# Weapon Upgrade System

Weapons can be upgraded at Sacred Shrines using Divine Essence collected during the journey.

## Level I

Basic weapon with standard attacks.

---

## Level II

Increased damage and durability.

---

## Level III

Unlocks new attack combos.

---

## Level IV

Adds divine energy effects and special abilities.

---

## Level V (Legendary)

Maximum weapon power with unique visual effects, enhanced finishers, and the highest combat potential.

# Design Philosophy

The weapon system of **SAVIOUR** focuses on quality rather than quantity. Each weapon serves a distinct gameplay purpose, encouraging players to switch between melee, ranged, and stealth combat while mastering the strengths of every weapon throughout the adventure.
