# Mission Design

## Overview

The campaign of **SAVIOUR** consists of a **Prologue** and **4 story-driven missions**. Each mission introduces new enemies, environments, gameplay mechanics, and boss encounters while advancing the story toward the final battle at the Tree of Souls.

---

# Prologue – The Chosen Guardian

## Story

Deep within a sacred mountain temple, ancient Gurus protect the legendary **Blue Bracelet**, waiting for the world's destined saviour. When a wild wolf accidentally touches the bracelet, it transforms into a powerful Wolf Guardian. The Gurus recognize the prophecy and begin training him to become the protector of the world.

## Objectives

* Learn movement and combat.
* Complete weapon training.
* Receive the Blue Bracelet.
* Attempt to lift the Divine Sword.
* Leave the Sacred Temple.

## Gameplay

* Tutorial
* Basic Combat
* Climbing
* Horse Riding
* Exploration

## Enemies

None

## Boss

None

## Reward

* Blue Bracelet
* Divine Sword
* Horse
* Basic Combat Skills

---

# Mission 1 – The Fallen Village

## Story

The Immortal King's soldiers invade a peaceful village searching for the stolen Golden Star. The village is destroyed and its people are massacred. The Saviour arrives just in time to rescue the last surviving child and confront the King's elite Crane Commander.

## Objectives

* Defeat the invading soldiers.
* Rescue the child.
* Protect the villagers.
* Escape the burning village.
* Defeat the Crane Commander.

## Gameplay

* Large-scale melee combat
* Archery
* Stealth
* Village exploration
* Rescue sequences

## Enemies

* Kingdom Soldiers
* Elite Soldiers
* Archers
* Crane Commander

## Boss

Crane Commander

## Reward

* Child joins the journey.
* Guardian Spear unlocked.

---

# Mission 2 – Whispering Jungle

## Story

The Saviour and the child travel into the Whispering Jungle in search of the **Emerald Fang**, a sacred relic that will awaken the Blue Bracelet. Along the way, they rescue a mythical Black Panther and face the powerful Beast Tamer.

## Objectives

* Protect the child.
* Rescue the Black Panther.
* Solve ancient temple puzzles.
* Obtain the Emerald Fang.
* Defeat the Beast Tamer.

## Gameplay

* Exploration
* Puzzle solving
* Horse riding
* Archery
* Boss battle

## Enemies

* Spear Guards
* Knife Assassins
* Beast Archers
* Jungle Guardians

## Boss

Beast Tamer

## Reward

* Emerald Fang
* Blue Bracelet Upgrade
* New Combat Skills

---

# Mission 3 – The Tree of Souls

## Story

The Saviour reaches the sacred Tree of Souls. The Immortal King sends his remaining Beast Warriors to stop him before he reaches the Golden Star. The child continues climbing the giant tree while the Saviour fights to protect him.

## Objectives

* Cross the Endless Waters.
* Defeat the Beast Warriors.
* Protect the child.
* Reach the Tree of Souls.

## Gameplay

* Multi-stage combat
* Exploration
* Platform traversal
* Elite enemy encounters

## Enemies

* Heavy Guardians
* Beast Archers
* Falcon Warrior
* Tiger Warrior
* Rhino Warrior
* Cobra Warrior
* Gorilla Warrior
* Panther Warrior
* Crocodile Warrior

## Boss

The Seven Beast Warriors

## Reward

* Full Blue Bracelet Power
* Divine Sword Awakens

---

# Mission 4 – The Last Saviour

## Story

At the heart of the Tree of Souls, the Immortal King demands the return of the Golden Star. The child refuses and climbs the tree while the Saviour confronts the King's final army. During the climactic battle, the Saviour destroys the Golden Star with the Divine Sword, releasing the imprisoned souls and ending the King's immortality forever.

## Objectives

* Defeat the Royal Commander.
* Protect the child.
* Reach the Golden Star.
* Defeat the Immortal King.
* Destroy the Golden Star.
* Release the imprisoned souls.

## Gameplay

* Large-scale final battle
* Multi-phase boss fight
* Cinematic combat
* Final escape sequence

## Enemies

* All previous enemy types
* Royal Commander
* Immortal King's Army

## Boss

The Immortal King

## Reward

* Humanity Saved
* Tree of Souls Restored
* Ending Cinematic

# Mission Progression

| Mission   | Location           | Main Objective             | Boss                 | Unlock Reward               |
| --------- | ------------------ | -------------------------- | -------------------- | --------------------------- |
| Prologue  | Sacred Temple      | Become the Chosen Guardian | None                 | Divine Sword, Blue Bracelet |
| Mission 1 | Fallen Village     | Rescue the Child           | Crane Commander      | Guardian Spear              |
| Mission 2 | Whispering Jungle  | Obtain the Emerald Fang    | Beast Tamer          | Bracelet Upgrade            |
| Mission 3 | Tree of Souls      | Defeat the Beast Warriors  | Seven Beast Warriors | Divine Sword Awakens        |
| Mission 4 | Tree of Souls Core | Defeat the Immortal King   | Immortal King        | World Saved                 |
