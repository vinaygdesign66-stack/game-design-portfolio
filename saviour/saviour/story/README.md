# Story

## Overview

**SAVIOUR** is a mythological action-adventure game that follows the journey of a divine guardian chosen by destiny to end the reign of an immortal king. Set in a world of ancient temples, sacred forests, and mystical kingdoms, the story explores sacrifice, courage, and the responsibility of protecting innocent lives.

---

# Opening Cinematic

Long ago, a ruthless king ruled his kingdom through fear and endless war. One day, he saved the life of a dying Rushi, who rewarded him with a divine blessing. After his death, the king was reborn with his life bound to the **Golden Star**, a sacred relic connected to the **Tree of Souls**.

As long as the Golden Star remained whole, the king could never die.

Believing himself to be invincible, he abandoned his kingdom and built an empire beneath the Tree of Souls, ruling through fear with the help of his immortal Beast Warriors.

Far away, ancient Gurus protected another sacred artifact—the **Blue Bracelet**—while waiting for the arrival of the world's destined saviour.

---

# Act I – The Burning Village

A giant eagle steals the Golden Star from the Immortal King and accidentally drops it into a peaceful village, where a young boy discovers it.

As the king's immortality begins to fade, he sends his army to recover the relic.

The village is attacked and destroyed.

The Saviour arrives too late to save everyone but rescues the last surviving child. After defeating the Crane Commander, he vows to protect the child and uncover the truth behind the Golden Star.

---

# Act II – The Whispering Jungle

The Saviour and the child journey into the mysterious Whispering Jungle in search of the **Emerald Fang**, a sacred relic capable of awakening the true power of the Blue Bracelet.

While exploring ancient ruins, they rescue a mythical Black Panther trapped by the king's soldiers.

Their actions draw the attention of the powerful **Beast Tamer**, guardian of the jungle.

After proving his courage and compassion, the Saviour defeats the Beast Tamer and earns the Emerald Fang, strengthening both himself and the Blue Bracelet.

---

# Act III – Journey to the Tree of Souls

Guided by the power of the Blue Bracelet, the Saviour and the child reach the legendary **Tree of Souls**, a colossal ancient tree standing in endless shallow water.

Knowing the heroes are close, the Immortal King sends his remaining Beast Warriors to stop them.

The Saviour battles through the King's strongest guardians while protecting the child, who begins climbing the Tree carrying the Golden Star.

Every victory brings them closer to ending the king's immortality.

---

# Act IV – The Final Battle

At the heart of the Tree of Souls, the Immortal King confronts the Saviour.

A fierce battle erupts beneath the giant tree as the child climbs higher with the Golden Star.

When the child throws the Golden Star toward him, the Saviour unleashes the full power of the Divine Sword and cuts the sacred relic in half.

The Tree of Souls cracks open.

Hundreds of imprisoned souls rise into the sky, finally freed after centuries of captivity.

Without the Golden Star, the Immortal King loses his immortality. The released souls surround him and pull him beneath the roots of the Tree, ending his reign forever.

His remaining Beast Warriors turn to dust, and peace returns to the world.

---

# Ending

The Tree of Souls is restored to its original beauty, its branches covered with green leaves once again.

The child buries the broken fragments of the Golden Star beneath the sacred tree.

The spirits of the ancient Gurus appear one final time to bless the Saviour before disappearing into the heavens.

After saying farewell to the child and the people he has protected, the Saviour mounts his horse and continues his endless journey, answering the call of anyone whose life is in danger.

---

# Sequel Hook

Although peace has returned, legends speak of other forgotten kingdoms, ancient relics, and powerful beings hidden beyond the known world.

With the Divine Sword on his back and the Blue Bracelet glowing on his arm, the Saviour rides toward a new horizon, ready to protect the innocent wherever darkness rises again.
